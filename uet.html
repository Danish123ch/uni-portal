About University of Engineering and Technology:

The University of Engineering and Technology (UET) Lahore is one of Pakistan’s oldest and most prestigious engineering institutions. Established in 1921 as Mughalpura Technical College, it has over a century of academic excellence and is recognized globally for its high standards in technical education. Located on G.T. Road near the historic Shalimar Gardens, UET Lahore is a primary hub for innovation and research. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs across seven faculties, including Electrical, Mechanical, Civil, and Chemical Engineering, as well as newer fields like Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity. In 2026, it continues to hold a top-tier position in both national and international (QS) rankings, specifically excelling in Engineering and Technology disciplines.

ABOUT VC:

Prof. Dr. Shahid Munir is the current Vice Chancellor of the University of Engineering and Technology (UET), Lahore, having been appointed to the post in September 2024. A highly accomplished academic, he holds a PhD in Chemical Engineering from the University of the Punjab and has an extensive background in energy and process technology. Before leading UET Lahore, he served as the Chairperson of the Punjab Higher Education Commission (PHEC) and as the Vice Chancellor of the University of Jhang. Since taking office, Dr. Munir has prioritized resolving the university's financial challenges, promoting environmental diplomacy, and aligning the curriculum with modern industrial requirements to ensure UET remains a premier institution for engineering excellence in Pakistan.
